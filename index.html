Privacy Policy

App: Azan (com.azanapp.azan) · Developer: Kemet-CA
Last updated: 23 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Azan mobile application ("the app", "we", "us") handles information. We have designed Azan to collect as little as possible. We do not run user accounts, we do not show ads, and we do not use analytics or tracking SDKs.

Information the app uses

  • Location (GPS). With your permission, the app uses your device's location to calculate accurate prayer times and the Qibla (direction to Mecca) for your area. Your coordinates are sent to the free, third‑party Aladhan prayer‑times API to retrieve the times for your location. We do not store your location on any server we control, and it is never used for advertising or tracking. Your most recent location is cached only on your own device so the app can work offline.
  • Local network access. If you choose to cast the Adhan to a Google Nest or Chromecast speaker, the app discovers compatible speakers on your local Wi‑Fi network and streams audio to them. This communication stays on your local network and is not sent to us.

Information we do NOT collect

  • No name, email, phone number, or account — the app has no sign‑in.
  • No advertising identifiers, no analytics, no third‑party trackers.
  • No contacts, photos, microphone, or files.

Data storage

Your settings (chosen Adhan sound, language, theme, prayer‑completion marks, and last known location) are stored only on your device using local storage. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

Third‑party service

The app requests prayer times from the Aladhan API over a secure (HTTPS) connection. Please refer to Aladhan's own terms for how they handle requests. No other third‑party service receives your data.

Children's privacy

Azan is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app contains no ads and no personal‑data collection.

Permissions summary

  • Location — prayer times & Qibla direction.
  • Notifications — prayer‑time reminders.
  • Local network / Bluetooth (discovery) — finding cast speakers.
  • Motion — the gentle tilt animation on the home panel (sensor data stays on the device).

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
